About

Description

This is an app about the Sungei Buloh Wetland Reserve.

It is First Singapore's ASEAN Heritage Park.

It was awarded a certificate by Wetlands International, marking the reserve's formal entry into the East Asian-Australasian Shorebird Site Network As from 2016, the park includes a 202ha of mangroves, mudflats, ponds and forests.
